Florentine

This Cheesy Florentine Pasta is a delectable, mouth-watering pile of yummy goodness! I recommend a nice salad with it — that's all you will need for a complete meal.

Recipe Project by Mitchell J Bourke

Ingriedients

  • 3 cups mostaccioli, uncooked
  • 1 pkg. (10 oz.) frozen chopped spinach
  • 4 oz. (1/2 of 8-oz. pkg.) PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, cubed
  • 1 cup 2% milkfat low fat cottage cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pkg. (8 oz.) KRAFT Shredded Low-Moisture Part-Skim Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1/4 cup KRAFT Grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions

Step 1

Heat oven to 375ºF.

Step 2

Cook pasta as directed on package, omitting salt. Meanwhile, cook spinach as directed on package; drain well. Place in large bowl. Add cream cheese; stir until melted. Stir in cottage cheese and eggs.

Step 3

Whisk Parmesan, cream, and egg yolks together in a medium bowl. Pour bacon mixture over pasta; stir in cream mixture. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 4

Drain pasta. Add to spinach mixture with the mozzarella; mix lightly.

Step 5

Spoon into 8- or 9-inch square baking dish; top with Parmesan.

Step 6

Bake 25 min. or until heated through.
